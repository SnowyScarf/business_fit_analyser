Why we built this
"Three students, one problem. No one could tell us which business actually made sense for us."

We watched batchmates start side-hustles based on what was trending — and struggle because the business didn't match their actual situation. Some had capital but no time. Some had skills but no market. Some had market but no money. BizFit was built to answer the question none of our seniors could answer properly: what fits you, specifically?

Our Mission

The right opportunity shouldn't be a privilege.

Every student hits the employment wall. But starting something of your own shouldn't mean blindly following trends or risking money you don't have. We built BizFit to strip away the guesswork and give you an honest, mathematical validation of what you can actually do with the resources you have today.
